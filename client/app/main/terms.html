Introduction

Splyt is a social music discovery experience built for the Google Chrome browser.

Accounts

To access Splyt, we require users log in with a Google+ account. Users can only create playlists and engage with other user content when registered and logged into the app. Logging in once will make your name and email address searchable to other users of the app. To deactivate your account and remove this information from any future search, please contact splytapp@gmail.com.
