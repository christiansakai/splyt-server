Discover

The chrome extension that will revolutionize the way you discover and listen to music. We take the work out of remembering and saving songs you come across during day-to-day web activities and store them in a convenient spot for you to access later.

Listen

Our web player supports Youtube, SoundCloud, and Tumblr audio content, allowing you to listen to all song formats from one app. Never worry about keeping separate open tabs for your different music streaming service accounts again!

Connect

Follow your friends and check out what they are adding to their own accounts. Show off your music taste and web browsing prowess by allowing your friends to access the latest additions to your playlists.

Part one. Extension.

Enjoy the full Splyt experience by installing our Chrome extension from the Chrome web store. Add songs to your playlists directly from the sites where you find them. Control the web app's player from any tab via the play/pause toggle in the extension popup or the keyboard shortcut: Alt+P. For more information about how to use the extension, watch our brief demonstration or check out the FAQ.

Part two. The web app.

Join Splyt by logging in with your Google+ account. Listen to songs you've added from wherever on the web. Create new playlists and follow friends. Reorder your playlists by dragging and dropping the song's labelled number. Search for new songs to add from Youtube and Soundcloud directly from within the app. Add songs to and from your own playlists and your friends' music stream. For more information about how to use the web app, watch our brief demonstration or check out the FAQ.
