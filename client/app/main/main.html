Discover

The chrome extension that will revolutionize the way you discover and listen to music. We take the work out of remembering and saving songs you come across during day-to-day web activities and store them in a convenient spot for you to access later.

Listen

Our web player supports Youtube, SoundCloud, Spotify and Tumblr audio content, allowing you to listen to all song formats from one app. Never worry about keeping separate open tabs for your different music streaming service accounts again!

Connect

Follow your friends and check out what they are adding to their own accounts. Show off your music taste and web browsing prowess by allowing your friends to access the latest additions to your playlists.
